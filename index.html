Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling, also alternatively translated as Only I Level Up, is a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. The Greatest Estate Developer

The Greatest Estate Developer is a Korean webnovel written by BK_Moon and ilustrated by Manggi. It was serialized on NAVER Series from June 3rd, 2019 to November 12th, 2020. A manhwa adaptation adapted by Lee Hyunmin and illustrated by Kim Hyunsoo began on August 5th, 2021 on NAVER Webtoon.

Lord of the Mysteries

Lord of Mysteries is a western fantasy (xuanhuan) web novel written by the Chinese author Cuttlefish That Loves Diving and published on QiDian. At the end of April 2020, the novel finished its eighth and final volume. The web novel was officially translated by CKTalon and published on Webnovel.

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint (ORV) is a novel by Sing Shong about Kim Dokja, an office worker who is the only reader of an obscure online novel called Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World. When the real world becomes similar to the novel, Kim's knowledge of the novel becomes essential to his survival. He teams up with the novel's protagonist, Yoo Joonghyuk, to change the plot and end the story more quickly.

SSS Class Suicide Hunter

SSSClass Suicide Hunter is a Korean web novel written by Shin Noah that ran from November 2018 to May 2020. The story is about Kim Gongja, who dies by suicide multiple times to become the world's greatest hunter. The novel is set in a future where humans have developed virtual technologies that allow them to see, touch, and do things in a magical world. The novel covers action, adventure, and comedy genres.

The Beginning After The End

The Beginning After the End (TBATE) is an English action fantasy novel written by TurtleMe (Brandon Lee) and illustrated by Fuyuki23. It was first published on Tapas in January 2017 and has 1,323 episodes. The story follows King Grey, who is reincarnated as Arthur Leywin on the continent of Dicathen, where he uses his military experience to become a mage.
